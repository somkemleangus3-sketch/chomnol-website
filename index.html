Chomnol (ចំណូល) App

Chomnol is a mobile application that allows users to upload, edit, and manage images with ease. Our goal is to provide a simple and safe platform for creative image interaction.

  • Permissions: Camera (to take photos), Storage (to upload images).
  • Data Storage & Security: Images are stored securely. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.
  • User Rights: You can contact us anytime to delete your data.
